This is Mohiuddin Ahmed, commonly known as Abid. I am a fresh Mechanical Engineering graduate from Bangladesh university of Engineering & Technology (BUET). During my 4 years Bachelors' studies, I explored the core Mechanical Engineering disciplines such as Fluidics, Thermal and Structural Engineering, and excelled in a handful of Software i.e. ANSYS Fluent, SolidWorks, COMSOL Multiphysics, MATLAB, Tecplot etc. My undergrad research was mostly centered on Computational Fluid Dynamics, turbulence, combustion and shock-induced hypersonic flow analysis. In addition, I have worked on the implementation of Control Theories in the Thermal Management of Micropolar Ferrofluids by utilizing different digital controllers. As a token of my professional experience, I have completed an Internship at the Electricity Generation Company of Bangladesh (EGCB), one of the leading power generation industries of Bangladesh.
Driven by a passion for advancing my knowledge in areas like CFD and Turbulence Modeling, I aim to expand my expertise in this field and materialize my understanding to address more practical challenges regarding Fluid Dynamics. Accordingly, I am currently seeking Ph.D. opportunities, particularly, in the realm of CFD, compressible fluids, or thermo-fluid system analysis in an esteemed institution.
