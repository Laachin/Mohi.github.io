Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE)

Student Affiliate Member, BUET

(July 2021   ~   July 2024)

I served as a Student Affiliate Member at the Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE), BUET, where I was actively involved in seminars, webinars, and other constructive programs. These experiences offered me a deeper understanding of the practical aspects of engineering, allowing me to engage with industry experts and peers alike. By participating in these events, I gained invaluable insights into current trends, best practices, and the ethical standards that define professional engineering. This involvement not only enhanced my technical knowledge but also played a crucial role in shaping my professional mindset, preparing me for future challenges in the field.

Badhan, BUET Zone

Executive Committee Member, Dr. M. A. Rashid Hall Unit

(May 2019   ~   June 2024)

I served as an Executive Committee Member of Badhan, BUET Zone, a humanitarian organization focused on promoting blood donation. This role allowed me to contribute to a cause that directly impacts the well-being of others, and it taught me the importance of sincerity and responsibility within my community, without seeking personal recognition. Through this experience, I learned to work collaboratively for the greater good, a lesson that I strive to carry forward in both my research and professional endeavors. It reinforced my commitment to service and instilled a deeper sense of social responsibility that I believe will guide me in my future career.
